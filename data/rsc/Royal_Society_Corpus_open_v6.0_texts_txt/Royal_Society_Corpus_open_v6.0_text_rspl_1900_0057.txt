PROCEEDINGS OF THE KOYAL SOCIETY .
June 21,1900 .
The LORD LISTER , F.R.C.S. , D.C.L. , President , in the Chair .
Mr. George Janies Burch , Dr. Leonard Hill , Mr. Joseph Jackson Lister , Professor Arthur A. Rambaut , Mr. William James Sell , Mr. Philip Watts , and Mr. Charles T. R. Wilson were admitted into the Society .
The Right Hon. Sir Ford North , a member of Her Majesty 's Most Honourable Privy Council , was balloted for and elected a Fellow of the Society .
A List of the Presents received was laid on the table , and thanks ordered for them .
