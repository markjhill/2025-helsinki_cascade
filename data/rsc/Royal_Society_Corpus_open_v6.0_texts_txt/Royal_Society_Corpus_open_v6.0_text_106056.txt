An Account of the Flowing of the Tides in the South Sea , as observed on board His Majesty 's Bark the Endeavour , by Lieut. J. Cook , Commander , in o Letter to Nevil Maskelyne , Astronomer Royal , and F. R. S. Mile-end , February 5 , 1772 .
Reverend Sir , I Here send you the few observations I made on the tides in the South Sea , to which I have only to add , that , from many circumstances and observations , I am fully convinced that the flood comes from the southward , or rather from the S. E. I am , SIR , Your most obedient , humble servant , J. Cook .
