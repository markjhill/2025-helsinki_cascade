XXII .
Journal of the Weather at Montreal .
By Mr. Barr .
Communicated by Richard Saunders , M. D. F. R. S. TO SIR JOHN PRINGLE , BART .
P. R. S. SIR , Brighton , 4th Sept. 1777 .
SOME days ago I received the enclosed Journal of the weather , kept by Mr. BAR R , for a few of the winter months , at Montreal , with a desire to put it into your hands .
I understand that the winter was thought particularly mild .
The thermometer was FAHRENHEIT'S graduated 50 degrees below 0.~ I observe that the wind never blew from the north ; nor do I know that it ever blew from that quarter while I was in America .
Read Feb. 26 , 1778 .
I have the honour to be , &amp;c. R. SAUNDERS .
