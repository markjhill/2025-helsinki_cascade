Particulars of a Fact , nearly similar to that related by Lord Morton , communicated to the President , in a Letter from Daniel Giles , Esq. Read November 23 , 1820 .
[ Phil. Trans. 1821 , p. 23 .
] In the litter of a black and white sow , by a boar of the wild breed , the chestnut colour of the boar strongly prevailed ; a second litter from the same mother , by a boar of a very different breed , retained many peculiarities of the wild breed ; and even in a third litter the chestnut colour was to a certain extent evident .
