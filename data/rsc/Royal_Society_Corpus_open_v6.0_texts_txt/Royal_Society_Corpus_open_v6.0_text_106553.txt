CHAPTER IV .
Of the Death or Recess of any Fellow .
UPON the Death , or the voluntary withdrawing , of any Fellow of the Society , one of the Secretaries shall note such their Death or Recess in the printed List of that year against their names ; and the Death , or withdrawing , of any Fellow shall be recorded in the Journal-book of the Society , among the Minutes of the proceedings of St. Andrew 's day .
