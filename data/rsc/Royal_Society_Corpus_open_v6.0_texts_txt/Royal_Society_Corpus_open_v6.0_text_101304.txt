A Suggestion For taking more notice , than hath been done formerly , of the Juyces of Trees , by tapping them .
ALthough some have been Curious in taking notice and making use of the Liquors of some Trees by tapping them , yet is there much wanting in this kind of Observations and Trials , als , in regard there have been upon this account but very few Trees examined , comparatively to those , that are yet left unconsidered and untried .
The Publisher therefore , being lately discoursed with upon this subject , and told , how great an addition there might be made to this knowledge , and how beneficial that might prove both for the preservation and recovery of Mans health , ( it being instanced to him , that one of the present Kings of Europe drinks much , and finds great benefit , of the juice of Walnut-trees ; ) thought himself obliged to recommend the enlargment of the said knowledge , by further Experiments , to all Ingenious and Industrious Men ; as also to request them , that they would please to impart unto him for public use , what they either already know , or shall hereafter discover and learn of
