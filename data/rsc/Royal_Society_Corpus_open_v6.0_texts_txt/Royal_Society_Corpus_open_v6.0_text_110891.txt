On the Barometrical Variation as affected by the Moon 's Declination .
" By Luke Howard , Esq. , F.R.S. In this paper , which is a continuation of that which was published in the Society 's Transactions for 1841 ( p. 277 ) , and in which the average results of the author 's observations extended only from the year 1815 to 1832 , similar records of calculated averages are given down to the year 1841 , and a comparison drawn with those of the former period .
The author places the whole of these data in the hands of the Royal Society , for the purpose of being made the basis of future inquiry , in what he terms " the nascent science of Meteo .
