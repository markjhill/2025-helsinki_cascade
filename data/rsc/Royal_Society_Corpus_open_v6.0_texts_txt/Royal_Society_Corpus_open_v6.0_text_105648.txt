A Letter from Benjamin Franklin , LL. D. and F. R. S. to the Reverend Thomas Birch , D. D. and Secretary to the Royal Society .
SIR , Craven-Street , Feb. 4 , 1762 .
MR. Canton did me the favour to show me the ingenious experiments he has described in the enclosed letter .
They succeeded perfectly as he has related them ; and I imagine , the communication of them must be agreeable to the curious in this branch of natural knowledge .
Read Feb. 4 , 1762 .
I am , SIR , With great respect , Your most obedient and most humble servant , B. Franklin .
