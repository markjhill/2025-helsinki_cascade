Hourly Observations of the Barometer and Thermometer at sea , on the 21st of March , 1840 .
By Major-General A. Lindsay , H.E.I.C.S. Communicated by Sir John F. W. Herschel , Bart. , V.P.R.S. These observations were made on board the ship Owen Glendower , on her voyage from Calcutta to London .
