" On Simultaneous Differential Equations in which the number of Variables exceeds by more than unity the number of the Equations .
" By GEORGE BOOLE , Esq. , F.R.S. Received June 19 , 1862 .
( Abstract .
) This paper contains the proof , with some applications , of a method described in a paper bearing nearly the same title which was published in the 'Proceedings of the Royal Society ' for March 6 , 1862 .
