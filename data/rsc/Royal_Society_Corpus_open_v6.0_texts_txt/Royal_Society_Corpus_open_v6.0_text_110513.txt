" Demonstration de l'egalite a deux droits de la somme des angles d'un triangle quelconque , independamment de la theory des paralleles , et de la consideration de l'infini .
" Par M. Paulet , de Geneve .
Communicated by P. M. Roget , M.D. , Sec. R.S. The author demonstrates the equality of the sum of the angles of a triangle to two right angles , by the aid of a preliminary theorem , of which the following is the enunciation .
A straight line forming an acute angle with another straight line , will , when sufficiently produced , meet any line , perpendicular to the latter , and situated on the side of the acute angle .
