" Meteorological Journal kept at Allenheads , near Hexham .
" By the Rev. Wiliiam Walton .
Communicated in a letter to P. M. Roget , M.D. , Sec. R.S. This Journal contains a register of the height of the barometer , taken at 9 a.m. and at 3 p.m. during every day in January and February 1836 , with remarks on the state of the weather during a few particular days .
The station where the observations were made is elevated 1400 feet above the level of the sea .
