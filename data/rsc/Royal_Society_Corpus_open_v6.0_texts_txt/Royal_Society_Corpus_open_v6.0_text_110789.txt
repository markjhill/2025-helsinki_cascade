" Boring Register , Bow Island , South Pacific .
" By Captain Edward Belcher , R.N. , communicated by Captain Beaufort , R.N. , F.R.S. The results of the boring operations carried on in this island are here given , as well as the register of the daily proceedings , under the particular superintendence of Mr. Thomas Pass , acting master of H.M.S. Sulphur .
The depth reached was 45 feet , when the augur broke , and no further progress could be made .
