June 1 , 1865 .
The Annual Meeting for the election of Fellows was held this day , Major-General SABINE , President , in the Chair .
The Statutes relating to the Election of Fellows having been read , Mr. Brayley and Dr. Webster were , with the consent of the Society , nominated Scrutators to assist the Secretaries in examining the Lists .
The votes of the Fellows present having been collected , the following Candidates were declared to be duly elected into the Society .
The Hon. James Cockle , M.A. Sir F. Leopold M"Clintock , Capt. Rev. William Rutter Dawes .
R.N. Archibald Geikie , Esq. Robert McDonnell , M.D. George Gore , Esq. William Kitchen Parker , Esq. Robert Grant , Esq. , M.A. Alfred Tennyson , Esq. , D.C.L. George Robert Gray , Esq.
