The observations , from which the results recorded in the present paper were made , are nearly 50,000 in number , and were taken four times each day , at the hours of 8 am .
noon , 4 and 8 p.m. The details of the observations themselves are about to be forwarded to the Royal Society ; they relate to the states of the barometer , thermome ?
ter , hygrometer , rain gauge , and the appearance of the atmosphere with regard to clearness or cloudiness .
