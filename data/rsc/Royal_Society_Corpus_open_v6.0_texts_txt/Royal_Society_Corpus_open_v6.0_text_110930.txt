" Description of a Process for extracting the Palladium which exists in combination with the Gold of the Gongosocho and other Mines in the Brazils .
" By Percival N. Johnson , Esq. , in a letter to the President , by whom it is communicated to the Society .
The process consists in melting the gold , obtained from its matrix by the ordinary processes of stamping and washing , with three times its weight of silver , granulating it in cold water , and refining it by the process of quartation , or separation of its alloys by nitric acid .
The nitric solution contains the silver , palladium , copper , and a small proportion of iron and lead .
