IV .
" On the Arrangement of the Muscular Fibres of the Ventricular Portion of the Vertebrate Heart ; with Physiological Remarks .
" By JAMES PETTIGREW , M.D. Communicated by Professor GOODSIR .
Received March 26 , 1863 .
This paper is a revised version of a previous one , having the same title , which was communicated to the Society on the 22nd of November , 1859 , and formed the substance of the Croonian Lecture delivered by the author on the 19th of April , 1860 .
The author has now inclucded the results of his researches on the structure of the ventricular portion of the heart in fishes , reptiles , and birds .
