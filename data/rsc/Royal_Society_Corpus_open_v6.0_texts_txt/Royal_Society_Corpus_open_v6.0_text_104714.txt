Extract of a Letter from Mr. Trembley F.R.S. to the President , concerning the same .
SIR , Harwich , Feb. 11 .
1749-50 .
THE Earthquake was not felt at Ingatstone , nor at Colchester , nor at this Town .
Read Feb. 15 1749-50 .
On Thursday last the Lightning fell on 2 Steeples not far from Colchester .
** * .
From ~ Your humble Servant ,
