III .
An Extraordinary high Tide in the River Thames observed by Capt. TH O. JONE S. MARCH the 8th , 1725-6 , The Tide in the River Thames , at New Crane in Shadwell , flowed twenty Foot , five Inches and a half , taken by a Level , from that High-water Mark , to Low-water the next Morning , and was four Inches higher than has been known these forty Years .
