The Success of the Experiment of Transfusing the Blood of one Animal into another .
THis Experiment , hitherto looked upon to be of an almost unsurmountable difficulty , hath been of late very successfully performed not only at Oxford , by the directions of that expert Anatomist Dr. Lower , but also in London , by order of the R. Society , at their public meeting in Gresham College : the Description of the particulars whereof , and the Method of Operation , is referred to the next Opportunity .
