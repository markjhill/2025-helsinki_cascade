Alloys of silver and copper possess many curious chemical and physical properties which make them interesting apart from their economic value , and entitle them to careful investigation .
The most remarkable of these s a molecular mobility , in virtue of which certain combinations of the ionstituents of a molten alloy become segregated from the mass , the lomogeneous character of which is thereby destroyed .
