A paper was also read , entitled , " Report of a remarkable appearance of the Aurora Borealis below the Clouds .
" By the Rev. James Farquharson , LL. D. , F.R.S. , Minister of Alford .
The phenomenon recorded in this paper occurred on the night of the 24th of February 1842 , when a remarkable aurora borealis was seen by the author apparently situated between himself and lofty stratus clouds , which extended in long parallel belts with narrow intervals of clear sky in a direction from north-west to south-east .
The author gives , in detail , the particulars of his observations .
