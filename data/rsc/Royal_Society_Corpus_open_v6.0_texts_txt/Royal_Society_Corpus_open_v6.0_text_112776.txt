June 2 , 1870 .
The Annual Meeting for the election of Fellows was held this day .
General Sir EDWARD SABINE , K.C.B. , President , in the Chair .
The Statutes relating to the election of Fellows having been read , Dr. Duncan and Capt. Evans were , with the consent of the Society , nominated Scrutators to assist the Secretaries in examining the Lists .
The votes of the Fellows present having been collected , the following Candidates were declared to be duly elected into the Society : William Froude , C.E. ' Rev. Stephen Parkinson , B.D. Edward Headlam Greenhow , M.D. Capt. Robert Mann Parsons , R.E. James Jago , M.D. William Henry Ransom , M.D. Nevil Story Maskelyne , M.A. Robert H. Scott , Esq. Maxwell Tylden-Masters , M.D. George Frederic Verdon , C.B. Alfred Newton , M.A. Augustus Voelcker , Ph. D. Andrew Noble , Esq. Samuel Wilks , M.D. Capt. Sherard Osborn , R.N. Thanks were voted to the Scrutators , 396 Presents .
