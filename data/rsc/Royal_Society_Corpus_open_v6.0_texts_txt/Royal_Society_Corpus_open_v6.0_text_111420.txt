XXIV .
" Supplementary Researches on the Partition of Numbers .
" By ARTHUR CAYLEY , Esq. , F.R.S. Received March 19 , 1857 .
( Abstract .
) The paper is supplementary to the author 's memoir , " Researches on the Partition of Numbers , " which comprises the two papers abstracts of which appear in the 'Proceedings ' of the Meeting of the 3rd of May , 1855 .
It contains some additional developments in relation to the theorem referred to at the conclusion of the former memoir , and an application to the determination of the expression for P ( 1 , 2 , 3 , 4 , 5 , 6 , ) 9 .
