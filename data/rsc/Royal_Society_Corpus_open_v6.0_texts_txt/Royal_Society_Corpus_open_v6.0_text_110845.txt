April 18 , 1844 .
The MARQUIS OF NORTHAMPTON , President , in the Chair , 1 .
Note in addition to Mr. Gassiot 's paper on the " Water Battery .
" The author here describes an instrument which he has recently constructed , and by means of which he is enabled with great facility , and without the aid of Zamboni 's pile , to test the tension in a single series of the voltaic battery .
