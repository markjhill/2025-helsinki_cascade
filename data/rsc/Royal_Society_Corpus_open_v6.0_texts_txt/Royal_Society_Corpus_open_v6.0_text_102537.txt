XI .
To give Iron the Colour and Tincture of Copper .
Communicated by Sir Roh .
Southwell , F. R. S. TAke of small thin Copper Pieces cleaned in the Fire ~ Aqua Fortis ~ being together in a Glass the Copper in Three or Four Hours will be dissolved , when it is cold you may use it , by washing with a Feather upon your Iron , that is presently made clean and smooth and it will presently take the Colour of Copper , when it comes out with much rubbing you may renew it again , but if you do it twice together , the Iron will look black .
