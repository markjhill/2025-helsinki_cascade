June 2 , 1864 .
The Annual Meeting for the Election of Fellows was held this day .
Major-General SABINE , President , in the Chair .
The Statutes relating to the Election of Fellows having been read , General Boileau and Sir Andrew Scott Waugh were , with the consent of the Societv , nominated Scrutators to assist the Secretaries in examining the lists .
The votes of the Fellows present having been collected , the following gentlemen were declared duly elected into the Society : Sir Henry Barkly , K.C.B. William Brinton , M.D. T.
