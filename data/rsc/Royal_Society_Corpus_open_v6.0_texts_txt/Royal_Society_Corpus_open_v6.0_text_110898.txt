" On the Theory of Vision , " in a letter to S. Hunter Christie , Esq. , Sec. R.S. By William Ford Stevenson , Esq. , F.R.S. The author adduces two experiments , of placing before the eye an object , the ends of which are marked , in a vertical position , as " clearly demonstrating that objects are not presented to the mind as they are found upon the retina , but in the actual position in which they are placed before the spectator .
