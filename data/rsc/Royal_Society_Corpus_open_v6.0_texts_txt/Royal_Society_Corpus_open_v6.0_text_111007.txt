" Researches on the Function of the Intercostal Muscles , and on the Respiratory Movements , with some remarks upon Muscular Power in Man .
" By Dr. John Hutchinson .
Communicated by Sir Benjamin C. Brodie , Bart. , F.R.S. This paper is an abridgement of a former one , bearing the same title , by the same author , which was read to the Society on the 17th of June , 1847 , and of which a full abstract is contained in the fourth volume of the ' Proceedings , ' p. 691 .
The long historical memoir , and quotations from former authors , have now been omitted .
