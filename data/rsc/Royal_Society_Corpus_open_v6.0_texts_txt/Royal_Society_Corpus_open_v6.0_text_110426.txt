" Observations with the Horizontal and Dipping Needles , made during a Voyage from England to New South Wales .
" By James Dunlop , Esq. Communicated by Capt. Beaufort , R.N. , F.R.S. This paper contains a very numerous and uninterrupted series of magnetical observations , made in the circumstances stated in the title , and extending about 180 degrees in longitude and 100 degrees in latitude .
The apparatus , of which a detailed description is given , was suspended from the roof of the cabin , and no alteration was made in its suspension from the beginning to the endof the voyage .
